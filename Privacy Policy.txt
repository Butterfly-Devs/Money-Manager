Privacy Policy – Money Manager App

Effective Date: October 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Money Manager App (“we,” “our,” or “the App”) collects, stores, and uses your personal information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that is necessary to provide the App’s services:

a. Information You Provide

Financial data: income, expenses, investments, loans/debts, donations, and money in hand.

Notes and contacts: any notes or contact information you choose to store in the app.

Account adjustments: balance updates and fund management records.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide and improve the App:

To store, organize, and display your financial data via Firebase cloud storage.

To enable synchronization across devices.

To generate reports, summaries, and analytics inside the App.

To troubleshoot issues and improve app functionality.

We do NOT:

Sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties.

Share your financial data with advertisers.

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored securely in Firebase Cloud Firestore.

Firebase provides industry-standard encryption and security for all stored data.

We implement additional security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

Important: No system is completely secure; while we make efforts to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cloud Firestore (for data storage)
